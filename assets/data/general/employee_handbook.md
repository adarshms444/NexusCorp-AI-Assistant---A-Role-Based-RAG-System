## Welcome & Introduction

### Company Vision and Mission
At **NexusCorp Analytics**, our vision is to empower businesses with actionable insights through AI-driven analytics. Our mission is to deliver innovative, data-first solutions that enable organizations to make smarter, faster decisions.

### Core Values
- **Integrity**: Transparent, ethical business practices.
- **Innovation**: Encourage creativity, experimentation, and continuous learning.
- **Collaboration**: Teamwork and knowledge-sharing are central to success.
- **Customer Focus**: Deliver measurable value to clients.
- **Accountability**: Own responsibilities and outcomes.

### Company Overview
Founded in 2018, NexusCorp Analytics is a leading analytics and AI insights company, specializing in predictive analytics, business intelligence, and AI-powered decision support. We operate globally with offices in multiple countries and a fully remote workforce.

## Leave Policies

### Types of Leave
| Leave Type | Entitlement/Details |
|------------|---------------------|
| **Privilege/Annual** | 15-21 days/year; accrued monthly |
| **Sick Leave** | 12 days/year; medical certificate for >2 days |
| **Casual Leave** | 7 days/year |
| **Maternity Leave** | 26 weeks (first two children); 12 weeks for subsequent children |
| **Paternity Leave** | 7-15 days (company policy) |
| **Bereavement Leave** | 3-7 days for immediate family |
| **Compensatory Off** | For work on holidays/weekends (manager approval required) |
| **Public Holidays** | 10-14 days/year; includes national & festival holidays |
| **Leave Without Pay** | Allowed with approval after exhausting paid leave |

### Leave Application Process
- Apply via HRMS/leave portal at least 3 days in advance (except emergencies).  
- Sick leave for >2 days requires a medical certificate.  
- Emergency leave must be communicated ASAP.  
- Approval from reporting manager and HR required.  
- Leave balance viewable on HRMS portal.

## Work Hours & Attendance

### Work Hours
- **Standard Hours**: 8 hours/day, 5 days/week (role-dependent).  
- **Flexible Timings**: Allowed for eligible roles with manager approval.  
- **Shift Work**: Applicable for some roles; shift allowance per policy.

### Attendance & Punctuality
- Mark attendance via HRMS app.  
- Notify manager of late arrivals/early departures.  
- 3+ late arrivals/month may trigger disciplinary action.

### Overtime & Compensation
- Overtime voluntary; paid at double regular wage rate.  
- Manager approval required.  
- Compensatory off may be provided in lieu.

## Code of Conduct & Workplace Behavior

### Professionalism & Respect
- Treat colleagues, clients, and vendors with respect.  
- Maintain an inclusive, harassment-free workplace.  
- Uphold confidentiality at all times.

### Anti-Discrimination & Equal Opportunity
- No discrimination based on gender, caste, religion, age, disability, sexual orientation, or other protected characteristics.  
- Equal opportunities in recruitment, promotion, and training.

### Dress Code
- Business casual Monday-Thursday; smart casual/ethnic Friday.  
- Avoid revealing or offensive clothing.

### Substance Abuse Policy
- No drugs or alcohol on company premises.  
- Violation may lead to termination and legal action.

### Harassment & Bullying Prevention
- Zero tolerance; complaints can be raised confidentially to HR or ICC.  
- Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) as per POSH Act.

### Conflict Resolution
- Attempt informal resolution first.  
- Escalate to HR or Grievance Redressal Officer if unresolved.  
- Formal complaints investigated and resolved within 30 days.

## Reimbursement Policies

### Eligible Expenses
- Travel: Air/train/bus fare (economy), local conveyance.  
- Accommodation: Hotel up to ₹3,000/night (bills required).  
- Meals: Up to ₹500/day on official travel.  
- Office Supplies: Manager-approved.

### Claim Process
1. Submit expense claims via HRMS with original bills within 30 days.  
2. Reviewed and approved by reporting manager.  
3. Processed with next payroll cycle.

### Documentation
- Original bills/invoices.  
- Travel tickets/boarding passes.  
- Approval emails (if pre-approved).

## Privacy & Data Security

- Compliance with Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.  
- Access restricted to authorized personnel.  
- Strong password policies, regular audits, employee training.  
- Confidential documents marked; shred/delete sensitive files as needed.

## Exit Policy

- Submit resignation in writing; notice period per appointment letter.  
- Termination for misconduct, underperformance, or business needs.  
- Severance as per statutory policy.  
- Return company assets before last working day.  
- Exit interviews conducted.  
- Full & Final settlement within 30-60 days.
